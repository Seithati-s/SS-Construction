Organisation overview

Founded in 2017, SS Construction is a construction company situated in Limpopo. Beginning with a single bakkie and a common work ethic, the business has developed into a reputable contractor that has finished more than 340 residential and commercial projects in North West and Limpopo. Renovations, plastering, tiling, new construction, and project management are among the services offered. SS Construction has a CIDB grade and is registered with the NHBRC.

In 2019, we expanded our services to include project management and structural consultations, broadening our capability to serve a wider range of client needs across the public and private sectors.
